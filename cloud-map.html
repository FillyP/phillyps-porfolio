Architectural Highlights

  • Inter-VLAN Routing: The Gateway Router utilizes sub-interfaces to route traffic between the isolated POS VLAN and the Back Office VLAN, allowing registers to check inventory without exposing the database to the public internet.
  • Broadcast Domain Isolation: Utilizing managed switches to confine broadcast traffic, drastically reducing network congestion and preventing unauthorized lateral movement.
